About

PlantCV is an imaging processing package specific for plants that is built upon open-source software platforms OpenCV 1, NumPy 2, and MatPlotLib 3. PlantCV was created at the Donald Danforth Plant Science Center in 2014. Please check back for new functions and tutorials. To more quickly serve your questions and issues please post them HERE
